About Ayurveda and Doshas

What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is an ancient Indian system of medicine that focuses on achieving balance between mind, body, and spirit. It's based on the concept of three fundamental energies or doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.

Vata Dosha

Vata is composed of air and ether elements. It governs movement, creativity, and communication. When balanced, Vata individuals are creative, quick-thinking, and adaptable.

  • Elements: Air + Ether
  • Qualities: Light, dry, cold, mobile
  • Functions: Movement, breathing, circulation

Pitta Dosha

Pitta is composed of fire and water elements. It governs metabolism, digestion, and transformation. When balanced, Pitta individuals are intelligent, focused, and natural leaders.

  • Elements: Fire + Water
  • Qualities: Hot, sharp, light, oily
  • Functions: Digestion, metabolism, intelligence

Kapha Dosha

Kapha is composed of earth and water elements. It governs structure, stability, and lubrication. When balanced, Kapha individuals are strong, loyal, and patient.

  • Elements: Earth + Water
  • Qualities: Heavy, slow, cool, oily
  • Functions: Structure, stability, growth
